CIRCLE

Circle around the current waypoint.
As the CIRCLE command can not know how long it should circle, this command should always be followed by a GOTO or UNTIL command.

Example: Circle until the altitude is higher than 100m

  1. CIRCLE(0m N, 0m E, Radius: 120m, Altitude: 120m)

  2. UNTIL (Altitude > 100m)

Example: Circle forever

  1. CIRCLE(0m N, 0m E, Radius: 120m, Altitude: 120m)

  2. GOTO (3)

Parameters:

  • Latitude of circle center

  • Longitude of circle center

  • Radius of circle. A positive radius makes the UAV circle clockwise, a negative radius counter-clockwise.

  • Target altitude
